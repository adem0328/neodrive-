Getting Started

How to Use NeoDrive

Your journey to intelligent mobility starts here. Follow these simple steps.

1

Initial Setup

Power on NeoDrive and complete the facial recognition setup. The system will scan and learn your profile for personalized experiences.

2

Calibrate Your Preferences

Set your driving preferences, comfort zones, and energy priorities. NeoDrive's AI will adapt and refine these settings as it learns your habits.

3

Enable V2X Communication

Activate vehicle-to-everything connectivity to join the smart network. Share and receive real-time road information with other vehicles.

4

Start Your Journey

Enter your destination and let NeoDrive optimize your route. The AI will select the most energy-efficient path based on your profile.

5

Monitor & Adapt

Review your energy usage, safety alerts, and trip analytics on the dashboard. NeoDrive continuously learns and improves from every journey.

6

Share Energy

When needed, connect with nearby NeoDrive vehicles for emergency energy transfer. Help others or receive assistance through the cooperative network.
